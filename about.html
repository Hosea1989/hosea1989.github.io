My Journey

My journey in the tech world began in 2016 when I decided to return to school, majoring in Computer Science at Los Angeles City College. My fascination with video games, technology, and tinkering led me to pursue a career in software development.

Now, as an Educator and Community Based Organizer for the Hidden Genius Project, I train and mentor black male youth in technology creation, entrepreneurship, and leadership skills. I believe that once fear is removed, anything can be achieved.

Outside of my tech pursuits, I enjoy playing video games, watching anime, and taking walks around the lake. I'm always excited to take on new challenges and contribute to innovative projects.
